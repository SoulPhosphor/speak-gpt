How to use this (click to expand)
  • This is a design tool. The side-by-side layout (controls on the left, phone on the right) is only how this tool works — the app itself will NOT look split-screened like this. The phone shape on the right is a preview of what the app will actually look like.
  • Dark / Light: you're designing two versions of each theme. Switch the toggle — the pickers and preview follow the mode you're editing.
  • Calm / Vibrant: same colors, two treatments. Calm = solid single-color edges, no glow (sensory-safe). Vibrant = multicolored gradient outlines + glow (the lively look). You design both — they share the same palette.
  • Pick a color: click a color square to open the full-spectrum picker, or type/paste an exact hex code. The preview updates the instant you change a color.
  • Not sure what a color affects? Hover (or tab to) any control and the exact spot it changes lights up with a dashed outline on the phone preview.
  • Save a theme: type a name, click Save. Your theme appears in the Saved Themes list below. Click any saved theme to load it back for editing. You can save as many themes as you want.
  • Copy for Claude: when you're happy with a theme, hit this button. It produces a readable list of all colors that you paste back in chat. That's how your chosen colors get into the app.
  • Reading surfaces (message bubbles, input box) default to charcoal (dark) or off-white (light) for readability. Color lives in the edges, glow, accents and buttons. You can change them, but readable defaults are there for a reason.
  • Reduce motion previews the still version (no animation), for overstimulation-sensitive design.

Pick colors, see them on two live phone previews, click around the menus. Nothing here changes the app.
How to use this (click to expand)
  • This is a design + layout check tool. The controls are on the left; the two phones on the right are a preview of what the app will actually look like. The app itself will NOT be split-screened — the two phones just show the same palette in two states at once.
  • Left phone = the slide-out drawer open (folders, chat list, bottom bar). Right phone = a normal chat. Both use the colors you pick.
  • You can click around (nothing is saved or deleted): click a chat to "open" it; long-press or right-click a chat, a folder, or the "Folders" row to see its menu; click "Folders" to expand/collapse; click a folder to drill into it (the back arrow returns).
  • Dark / Light: you're designing two versions of each theme. Switch the toggle — pickers and preview follow the mode you're editing.
  • Calm / Vibrant: same colors, two treatments. Calm = solid single-color edges, no glow (sensory-safe). Vibrant = multicolored gradient outlines + glow.
  • Pick a color: click a color square for the full picker, or type/paste a hex code. The preview updates instantly.
  • Not sure what a color affects? Hover (or tab to) any control and the exact spot it changes lights up with a dashed outline on the phones.
  • Save a theme: name it, click Save — it appears in Saved Themes below. Click any saved theme to load it for editing.
  • Copy for Claude: when happy, hit this and paste the result back in chat. That's how your colors get into the app.
  • Reduce motion previews the still version (no animation).
